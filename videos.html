Convert Gas Fireplace into Stock during Power Loss in Texas

On the 3rd day of the winter storm hitted Texas, we lost electricity power in our community and neighborhood, and started to have cooking trouble even if you have food: No microwave; no eletric stove. Then I uploaded my first Youtube video to show people my experience and tips of converting the fireplace into a stove for cooking. Got > 1.2k views and hope I really helped some people in need.
